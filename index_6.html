Our Mission

Why We Study

The Rocket Study Club was founded by a group of dentists who believe that learning doesn't stop at graduation. As an international peer-led study club, we gather regularly to challenge ourselves, stay current with the evidence, and hold each other accountable to the highest standard of patient care.

Our format blends invited expert lectures with member-led literature reviews, case presentations, and open discussion — creating a space where curiosity is rewarded and questions are always welcome.

Whether you're a recent graduate or a seasoned clinician, there is always something new to learn and someone to learn it with.

Evidence-Based Practice
We anchor every discussion in current literature and clinical research, fostering critical appraisal skills across all members.
Collegial Community
We prioritize a culture of psychological safety — where honest questions and respectful debate are always encouraged.
Clinical Excellence
Case-based learning connects theory to practice, helping members grow as both clinicians and diagnosticians.
Accessible Format
Hybrid meetings (in-person + Zoom) ensure participation is possible regardless of geography or schedule.
